Last Updated: June 23, 2025

Introduction

At Newsak, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application Newsak. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use the App:

  • Identifiers: Device identifiers, advertising identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS), and other unique identifiers assigned to your device
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including pages viewed, time spent in the app, app features used, and in-app actions
  • Personal Information: Information such as your name, email address (if provided)

Data Used to Track You

  • Usage Data: Your app usage patterns and behavior data that may be linked with data from other apps and websites for advertising purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality

  • To comply with legal obligations

Advertising and Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App. AdMob automatically collects and processes the following data:

  • Device identifiers including advertising ID (IDFA on iOS)
  • Usage data including app interactions and behavior patterns
  • Device information such as device type, operating system, and app version

This data is used for:

  • Delivering targeted advertisements
  • Measuring advertising effectiveness
  • Cross-app and cross-website tracking for advertising purposes

Regional Advertising Policies

  • In most countries, we may show personalized ads to improve relevance and user experience.
  • In the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom (UK), we do not show personalized ads unless you have provided explicit consent. If no consent is given, only non-personalized ads are shown in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS devices, we will request your permission before tracking your data across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes. You can grant or deny this permission, and you can change your choice at any time in your device settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking.

To learn more about how Google uses information from apps like ours, please see Google's Partner Policy.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following cases:

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Advertising partners may retain data according to their own retention policies.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal exceptions

Opting Out of Targeted Advertising
